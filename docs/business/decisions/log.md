### DEC-001: CRM Platform Selection
**Date:** 2024-XX-XX
**Category:** Technology
**Decision Maker:** Sharon

**Context:**
Needed a CRM system to manage tax refund leads and track client cases.

**Options Considered:**
1. Off-the-shelf CRM (Monday, Zoho) - Easy setup, but limited customization
2. Custom-built CRM (Next.js + Supabase) - Full control, tailored to workflow
3. Spreadsheets - Simple but doesn't scale

**Decision:**
Build custom CRM with Next.js and Supabase. Provides full control over features, no monthly per-user fees, and can be tailored exactly to tax refund workflow.

**Expected Outcome:**
Efficient lead management with Israel-specific features like WhatsApp integration.

**Actual Outcome:**
CRM operational with lead tracking, status management, WhatsApp integration, and playbooks.

**Lessons Learned:**
Custom development takes more time upfront but pays off in flexibility.

### DEC-003: Commission-Based Pricing Model
**Date:** 2024-XX-XX
**Category:** Finance
**Decision Maker:** Sharon

**Context:**
Need to determine pricing strategy for tax refund services.

**Options Considered:**
1. Fixed fee per case - Simple but may not align with value delivered
2. Commission (% of refund) - Aligns incentives, client pays only on success
3. Hourly rate - Common but hard for clients to budget

**Decision:**
Commission-based pricing (% of refund amount). Zero risk to client, payment only when refund received.

**Expected Outcome:**
Higher conversion rate due to no upfront cost, aligned incentives.
